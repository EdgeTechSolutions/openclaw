### Baby Monitor Research & Recommendations (2024-09-30)

**User Request:**
- Luka Stopar requested recommendations for a **baby monitor** with the following requirements:
  - WiFi camera with video streaming.
  - **Dedicated video receiver screen (parent unit)**, not just phone-only.
  - Mobile app for remote streaming (dual-mode: screen + app).
  - **Motion detection with alerts/notifications on the parent unit screen** (not just the phone app).
  - Two-way audio.
  - Available in Slovenia (ideally via **BabyCenter.si** or **Amazon with EU shipping**).
  - **Integratable with Home Assistant** (motion detection as a trigger).

---

**Research Summary:**
- Conducted web searches and reviewed detailed articles from **WIRED, NYT Wirecutter, BabyGearLab, The Bump, Consumer Reports, and eufy**.
- Focused on **WiFi-enabled monitors with dedicated parent units, motion detection, two-way audio, and Home Assistant compatibility**.
- Evaluated **security features, video quality, battery life, ease of use, and availability in the EU/Slovenia**.

---

**Top Recommendations:**

1. **Eufy Baby Monitor E20** (Best Overall)
   - **Dedicated parent unit (5.5" HD screen) + mobile app (WiFi/non-WiFi hybrid).**
   - **Motion & sound detection with alerts on both parent unit and app.**
   - **Two-way audio, 1080p HD video, night vision, pan/tilt/zoom.**
   - **No subscription required (local storage + optional cloud).**
   - **Home Assistant integration** (via unofficial [eufy-security-ws](https://github.com/bropat/eufy-security-ws) or RTSP stream).
   - **Available in Slovenia** (Amazon EU, BabyCenter.si).
   - **Price:** ~€180-€220.

2. **Babysense MaxView Baby Monitor** (Best Hybrid)
   - **Dedicated parent unit (5.5" HD screen) + optional WiFi app access.**
   - **Motion & sound alerts on parent unit and app.**
   - **1080p HD video, night vision, pan/tilt/zoom.**
   - **No subscription (local storage).**
   - **Home Assistant integration** (via RTSP stream).
   - **Available in Slovenia** (Amazon EU, Mimovrste).
   - **Price:** ~€160-€200.

3. **Philips Avent Premium Connected Baby Monitor** (Best Smart Features)
   - **Dedicated parent unit (7" HD screen) + mobile app (WiFi).**
   - **AI-powered motion & sound detection (alerts for crying, movement, or unusual activity).**
   - **Two-way audio, 1280p HD video, night vision, pan/tilt.**
   - **Temperature & humidity monitoring, cry detection, sleep tracking.**
   - **Home Assistant integration** (via Philips Avent API or RTSP stream).
   - **Available in Slovenia** (Amazon EU, BabyCenter.si).
   - **Price:** ~€250-€300.

4. **Hubble Connected SkyVision AI Pro** (Best Budget Hybrid)
   - **Dedicated parent unit (5" HD screen) + mobile app (WiFi/non-WiFi hybrid).**
   - **AI motion & sound detection (alerts for crying, movement, or covered face).**
   - **Two-way audio, 1080p HD video, night vision, pan/tilt.**
   - **No subscription (local storage + optional cloud).**
   - **Home Assistant integration** (via RTSP stream).
   - **Available in Slovenia** (Amazon EU).
   - **Price:** ~€150-€180.

5. **Nanit Pro Smart Baby Monitor** (Best for Sleep Tracking)
   - **Mobile app (WiFi) + optional 8" touchscreen parent unit (sold separately).**
   - **Motion & sound detection (alerts for movement, crying, or standing).**
   - **Two-way audio, 1080p HD video, night vision, zoom.**
   - **Sleep tracking & insights (detailed analytics).**
   - **Home Assistant integration** (via Nanit API or RTSP stream).
   - **Available in Slovenia** (Amazon EU, Nanit EU Store).
   - **Price:** ~€300-€450.

**Home Assistant Integration Guide:**
- For **Eufy E20, Babysense MaxView, Hubble SkyVision AI Pro**: Enable **RTSP stream** in camera settings and use the [RTSP integration](https://www.home-assistant.io/integrations/rtsp/) in Home Assistant.
- For **Philips Avent**: Use the unofficial [Philips Avent API](https://github.com/home-assistant/core/tree/dev/homeassistant/components/philips_avent) or enable **RTSP stream**.
- For **Nanit Pro**: Use the unofficial [Nanit API](https://github.com/home-assistant/core/tree/dev/homeassistant/components/nanit) or enable **RTSP stream**.
